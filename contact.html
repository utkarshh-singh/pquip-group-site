FAQ

Do I need an appointment to visit?

Yes—please email us in advance. NRC facilities require visitor clearance and sign-in at reception.

Where should I send application materials?

Follow the instructions on Join. For general interest, one PDF (CV, statement, transcripts) is ideal.

Do you host short-term research visits or internships?

Yes—summer, co-op, and visiting internships are common. Please reach out 3–5 months in advance.
